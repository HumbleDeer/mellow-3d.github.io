If you are unsure open the resource manager to view the drive letter of the SD card {% include image.html file=belana_img2 url=belana_url2 alt=belana_cap2 caption=belana_cap2 %} - Click "Flash", when the progress bar appears it will start writing the system image to the SD card - Wait for the flash to complete, you can skip the verification step and eject the SD card - The SD card can now be inserted into the {{boardname}} - Your {{boardname}} is ready to start up.
